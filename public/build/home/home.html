How Does Hatch Work?

1
Our designer provides us information about his/her product and the minimum # of orders required to unlock the discounted group-order price.
2
Our community of design enthusiasts, one by one, commit to buying the product, and collectively hatch the product by meeting its minimum order.
3
Once the minimum order is met, our designer go into manufacturing and prepare to ship the goods to you.
