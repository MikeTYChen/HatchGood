Cora Neil is a Los Angeles-based environmental designer specializing in furniture, lighting, and spatial design that makes a social impact and inspires good living. Her work has been recognized by the Donghia Foundation, the Spark Awards, the Dell Social Innovation Challenge, featured in Frame Magazine, and she was invited to speak at Dwell on Design about social innovation in education. She has studied fashion, printmaking, ceramics, and metal working, and she's a graduate of the Art Center College of Design.
